description: The osprey (Pandion haliaetus), also called sea hawk, river hawk, and fish hawk, is a diurnal, fish-eating bird of prey with a cosmopolitan range. These birds are the big kingfisher, diving into bodies of water to catch fish. Osprey visit the UK from Africa in Summer to breed. The best places to see these birds are in the Scottish Highlands, but you can see them in England as the move up the country.
