Why Buy From Me?

Exclusive & Flexible

Every print is one of a kind — you'll be the only person who ever owns a printed copy, backed by a certificate of exclusivity. Choose to receive a physical print shipped to your door, or a full resolution digital file with a print release for unlimited personal printing.

Local Photographs

Each image comes from somewhere meaningful — somewhere you might know, love, or have stood yourself. I place emphasis on connecting local people with photographs of local places.

Prints For Conservation

20% of every sale is donated to a conservation charity relevant to the species or landscape in the photograph. You take the credit for the donation if you wish — I just facilitate it.
