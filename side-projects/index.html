Nature Squamish: Bird Count Portal

The Nature Squamish Monthly Bird Count is a community even run by Nature Squamish each month in the Squamish Estuary. Since the count started in 1991, over 400 months of bird observation data has been collected by volunteers over many thousands of hours. I created this data portal to take this data in it's raw form, and make it accessible to anyone who is interested to browse and explore.

Nature Walks

The Skwelwil'em Squamish Estuary is one of British Columbia's most important wildlife areas. Supporting over 200 species of wintering and migrating birds, alongside salmon, black bear, black-tailed deer and several species at risk, this area is a biodiversity hotspot at the heart of the Coast Mountains.
