Niall Bell

I'm Niall, a nature photographer and environmentalist from the UK, based in British Columbia, Canada. I spend my time documenting the wildlife and wild places of my surroundings and sharing the beauty of the natural world through photography and writing.

Welcome

niallbell.com is the digital home of my photography, videography, and writing. I built and maintain the website myself in my spare time with the hope of sharing inspiring photographs and interesting writing.

In an era of social media and AI, human visitors to independent websites are becoming increasingly rare, so thank you for being here.

Learn more about how your visit matters →

I worry that as AI crawlers scrape the web and funnel content into chatbots, the motivation to maintain sites like this will slowly fade away. I've built measures into this site to block those crawlers, because the work I put in here was never meant to train a model. I don't rely on ad revenue, and I don't even serve ads, but many websites do. Without real human visitors, those sites don't get paid, and when they don't get paid, they're forced to find other ways to make money, which puts strain authentic writing. If these values resonate with you, consider browsing the web a little more directly, and let the surfing be yours. Thank you.
