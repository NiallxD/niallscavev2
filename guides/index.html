Squamish, British Columbia

I'm currently offering guided wildlife watching walks and Photography Workshops in Squamish, British Columbia while living and working in this wonderful mountain-town. This is a magical place where land, lakes, rivers, mountains, forests, and the ocean meet in an explosion of incredible scenery and biodiversity.

Why Walk With Me?

Local Expertise

The Skwelwil'em Squamish Estuary is one of BC's most important wildlife areas, supporting over 200 bird species, salmon, black bear, and species at risk. Spending many hours each week exploring this area has given me intimate knowledge of this connected estuarine ecosystem, I know where to look and when.

Ethical Observation

Every nature walk is led with a non-intrusive, wildlife-first approach. We move slowly, observe carefully, and leave the area just as it was when we arrived.

Free & Informal

These are relaxed, free of charge guided bird walks for anyone who wants to explore. I'm grateful to be able to explore this wonderful area, and I love little more than sharing it with others. So whether you're a seasoned birder or have never held a pair of binoculars, come join a walk when you're here in Squamish.

What To Expect

The Walk

2-5 Hours

We'll walk the Squamish estuary at a slow, deliberate pace, with lots of pausing to watch, listen, and identify what is around us. I'll share what I know about the birds, the landscape, the seasons, and how this incredible system is connected.

  • Flexible timing: dawn light or golden hour evenings
  • Bird and wildlife identification throughout
  • Insights into behaviour and ecology of Squamish's wildlife
  • Completely free, no booking required. Just get in touch

The Season

Year Round

Squamish has lots to offer regardless of the season. In winter, hundreds of bald eagles gather to feed on spawning salmon, and in spring, tens of thousands of migratory birds arrive.

  • Peak bald eagle watching Nov–Jan
  • Wintering waterfowl and shorebirds
  • Migrating warblers and hummingbirds
  • Guided walks in Squamish available year-round

Why Workshop With Me?

1:1 Tutoring

1:1 tutoring as standard here in Squamish. You won't spend the day waiting for your turn. Every session is built around you, your camera, and what's in front of us.

Entirely In The Field

Over ten years photographing wildlife across three continents. I teach what I actually do. Reading situations, best ethical approaches, anticipating behaviour. Not what a textbook says.

Tailored to You

No fixed workshops. Whether you've just bought your first camera or want to sharpen a specific skill, we focus entirely on what will move your photography forward.
