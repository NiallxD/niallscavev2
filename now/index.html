Just for fun

Canada

My partner and I are exploring Canada — Squamish, BC is home for now. Much of our time is spent out in the mountains and wilderness exploring.

📷

Photography

If you've browsed any other page on this site you'll know my hobsession* is nature photography. I'm making a conscious effort to get out with the camera more — it makes me happy.

📖

Exploring Philosophies

I've spent time reading about Stoicism and Epicureanism. Next up is a deeper dive into Buddhism and Taoism. Ancient philosophy is endlessly fascinating.

🧠

Managing Self

My brain works a bit differently — neurodivergent, ADD/ADHD, pick your label. I've started emptying my brain into notes so I can focus on the important things. So far it's working… I think.

* A Hobby one is Obsessed with — a portmanteau I'm rather pleased with.
