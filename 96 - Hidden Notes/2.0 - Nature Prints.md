## Why Buy From Me?

This is a valid question, I'm not a big name photographer but I hold my images in high regard. Below are some of the reasons why I think I offer great print options to clients.

> [!tip]- Exclusive Print Promise
> I believe that there is value in exclusivity when it comes to art. In our modern world one can pickup a general photograph of something cool from IKEA for next to nothing. While the photographer behind these prints are talented for sure, the buyer doesn't purchase a unique piece. This is why I opted to a adopt an Exclusive Print Promise. If you buy one of my prints, you can rest assured you will be the only person who has a printed copy of that photograph, and you'll get a certificate to prove it.

> [!tip]- Local Photographs
> I place emphasis on providing photographic prints of local places to local people. I have spent time in many places around the world, and I've created many photographs I'm super proud of. I want to get prints of these photographs into the homes and businesses of people who connect to these places/creatures.

> [!tip]- Prints For Conservation
> I feel truly honoured to be able to photograph some of the most exquisite creatures we share our home with, and I only feel it's right to give something back when I sell a photographic print of them. I wouldn't get away with selling a print of Elvis without paying royalties, so why should I sell a print of a dolphin without giving something back? 
> 
> This philosophy lead me to my Prints For Conservation scheme in which I donate a percentage of the proceeds of each print to a conservation charity relevant to the species/landscape in the photograph. I typically donate 20% of the proceeds to the charity and the purchaser gets the credit if they wish.

> [!tip]- Printing That Works For You
> I think flexibility is important and I like to offer clients a choice on how they receive their prints:
> 
>>**Option 1:** I can print and ship the photograph to you using several online print labs.
>>**Option 2:** I can provide you with a full resolution digital photograph file and print
>> release agreement so you can print the photograph yourself. 
>
>Option 2 comes with the flexibility of unlimited personal use prints.
